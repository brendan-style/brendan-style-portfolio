Home Run Betting Model

Published

July 9, 2025

This will be a publishing of my Home Run predictor model that I created for my Data Analytics Capstone during my senior year of college. The model will, if given information about a hitter’s matchup, produce a rating. Eventually, these ratings will be associated with a predicted percentage chance, and thus have predicted odds, but currently these odds will not be present, as I do not yet have enough data to predict those. In it’s place we will have the odds assigned by FanDuel and DraftKings. Some things to note:

  • Players season long stats will be updated every Thursday
  • Hitters facing pitchers with insufficient data will be left off the list
